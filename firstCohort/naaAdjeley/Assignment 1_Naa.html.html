SCOPE

This recommended practice supplements the standard by providing inspectors with information that can improve skill and increase basic knowledge of inspection practices. It describes these practices for all applicable instruments. Common components, joining methods, inspection planning processes, inspection intervals and techniques, and types of records are described to aid the inspectors in fulfilling their role implementing the standard.
